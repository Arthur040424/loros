The Letter

🌻 ✦
📅 Today & Always
Hey bestie,

I keep trying to find the right words for this, and honestly? I don't think there are any. Some things just feel too big for sentences. But I'm going to try anyway, because that's what you do for people who matter — you try.

You walked into my life and just… stayed. Not in a loud way. In that quiet, reliable, steady way that's actually so much more rare. You're the person I call when something hilarious happens at 11pm and also the person I call when everything is falling apart at 2am. And somehow you always pick up.

I love the way we don't need to explain ourselves to each other. The inside jokes that make zero sense out of context. The conversations that start about nothing and end up being the most meaningful things I've heard all week. The fact that we can sit in comfortable silence and it doesn't feel weird — it feels like home.

Well, Lorraine, you are really one difficult girl to understand, you at times can be really really annoying. You surely do know how to just make me get mad at you, even though you usually end up not realizing it. But oh, well, I guess no matter how much I tell myself I'm mad at you, I just can't stay mad at you for too long.

You've seen me at my absolute worst. The messy versions, the overthinking versions, the "I know I should let it go but I genuinely can't" versions. And you're still here. That's not nothing. That's everything.

I just wanted you to have something that says: you are seen, you are appreciated, you are loved and I am genuinely so lucky that the universe decided we should be in each other's lives. You are not supposed to be hurt by other people that don't appreciate you, or be in pain because of something that is affecting you. I want to be the first person you call whenever you feel down, I want to be the person you run to whenever you need a shoulder to cry on, I want to be the person you call when you're happy and you want to share it with someone. You are supposed to be loved and cherished and I hope you never forget that.

— your person, King Arthur always 💛
P.S. You don't get to not be my best friend. That ship has sailed. Sorry (not sorry).
